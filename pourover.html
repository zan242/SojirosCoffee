Pour Over

Pour overs are great for people trying to make a smooth cup of coffee.

How to Brew

  1. Heat your water to 205 degrees. (Not boiling, since that will burn your grounds!)
  2. Measure out 25 grams of coffee beans.
  3. Put your filter in your pour over cone and wet it to remove the paper taste.
  4. Grind your beans to a medium consistency using a burr grinder.
  5. Discard the water that you used to rinse your paper filter.
  6. Add your grounds to the pour over cone and pour enough water to saturate your grounds. (Pour in a spiral motion to evenly saturate your grounds.)
  7. After waiting 30 seconds, slowly pour another 400 grams of water for two minutes.
  8. Pour your coffee into a cup and enjoy.
