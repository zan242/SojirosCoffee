Aeropress

An Aeropress is great for people trying to make one, clean cup of coffee and also need some portability.

How to Brew

  1. Heat your water to 190 degrees. (Not boiling, since that will burn your grounds!)
  2. Measure out 18 grams of coffee beans.
  3. Put your filter in the aeropress' basket and wet it to remove the paper taste.
  4. Grind your beans to a medium-fine consistency using a burr grinder.
  5. Discard the water that you used to rinse your paper filter.
  6. Add your grounds to the brew chamber and pour enough water to reach the number 4 marking. (Spin your chamber while pouring to evenly saturate your grounds.)
  7. Stir the slurry and then place your plunger on the aeropress.
  8. After a minute and a half, stir the slurry again and then gently press the plunger down until you hear a hissing sound.
  9. Enjoy.
