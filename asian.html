Asian Coffee

Asian Coffee is known for being earthy and chocolaty. It has the sweetest flavors out of the regions.

Highwire Batak, Sumatra

This medium roast coffee has the common earthy and chocolaty notes found among asian coffees, but also has a hint of fruit. The coffee has a very heavy body and is fairly acidic, which is pretty unique for a medium roast from Asia. You can buy it here.

Caffe Vita Theo Blend

This blend has a very chocolaty flavor and aroma along with a little bit of frutiness and vanilla. It has a full body and is slighty acidic. This blend was developed with a chocolate company and as a result pairs very well with chocolate. You can buy it here.

Red Rooster Funky Chicken

This blend combines an earthy sweetness with some brightness and acidity to create a very complex flavor. It is a medium roast, but is brighter and full bodied, which are usually indicitave of light and dark roasts. You can buy it here.
