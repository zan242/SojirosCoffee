American Coffee

American Coffee is known for its spicy and nutty qualities. It is the most balanced coffee in terms of flavor, not being too acidic or sweet.

Brazil Serra Do Cigano

This Coffee has a very funky taste. It's a light roast that is both nutty and fruity. It's sweeter than most American coffees and has a slightly fuller body than most light roasts. All around this coffee is a bit of a wild card, but is excellent none the less. You can buy it here.

Madcap Corazon Peru

This is one of my favorite coffees. Its very floral and has notes of pineapple and blueberry with caramel undertones that all combine to make a sweet and tart taste. It has a medium body and fruity aftertaste that will leave you wanting more. You can buy it here.

PT's Flatlander Signature Blend

This South American Blend has a nutty, dark chocolaty flavor and has a strong bittersweet chocolate aroma. It's a medium roast, but the flavor of the coffee still comes through and mixes well with the flavor of the roast. You can buy it here.
