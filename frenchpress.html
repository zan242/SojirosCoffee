French Press

French Presses are great for people trying to make multiple, clean cups of coffee.

How to Brew

  1. Heat your water to 205 degrees. (Not boiling, since that will burn your grounds!)
  2. Measure out 55 grams of coffee beans.
  3. Grind your beans to a medium-coarse consistency using a burr grinder.
  4. Add your grounds to the press and pour enough water to saturate your grounds. (Pour in a spiral motion to evenly saturate your grounds.)
  5. After waiting 30 seconds, pour another 770 grams of water.
  6. Press the plunger down into the press so it touches the top of the water.
  7. After 4 minutes pour your coffee into a cup and enjoy. (You can go 3-5 minutes depending on how strong your want you coffee, but 4 is average.)
