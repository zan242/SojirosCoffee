African Coffee

African Coffee is known for being bright and acidic. They commonly have fruity and citrusy flavors.

Equator Ethiopia Sidama Ardi

This light roast coffee contains notes of vanilla, lavender, and lemon. It tastes like no other coffee I've ever had by a long shot. I highly recommend this if you want to be more adventurous with your coffee choices.

Necessary Ethiopia

This is a medium roast with a light fruity taste of plum and lemon. It isn't as fruity and acidic as many other African coffees so its a good place to start if you don't want anything too different.

Buy Here

Gimme! Darkness

This dark roast blend tastes like bittersweet chocolate with toasty undertones. There is a toastiness and smokiness to the coffee since it's such a dark roast and it has a silky mouthfeel when drinking.
